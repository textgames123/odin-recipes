Corn on the Grill

Description

Make some delicious grilled corn in 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • 6 ears fresh corn
