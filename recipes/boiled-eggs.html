Boiled Eggs Recipe

Description

Make the most easy boiled eggs in 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Carefully place an egg into each cup of a standard muffin tin.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  4. Transfer baked eggs to a large bowl filled with ice water. Let sit until cooled completely, about 10 minutes.
  5. Enjoy your eggs!
