Pancake

An delicious pancake made in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; Make a well in the center. Pour in milk, oil, and egg; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop about 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto the griddle; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip; Cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  4. Enjoy your pancake!
